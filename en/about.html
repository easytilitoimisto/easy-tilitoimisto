OUR HISTORY

Building Trust in Finnish Accounting

Founded in 2010, Easy Tilitoimisto began with a simple mission: to make professional accounting accessible for small businesses and freelancers in Finland.

What started as a two-person operation in Helsinki has grown into a team of 12 accounting professionals serving over 500 clients nationwide. We've remained committed to our core values of transparency, accuracy, and personalized service.
